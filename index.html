External USB Charger

For $21 I went with the Anker Astro E3 Portable Charger. A simple and inexpensive addition to my gadgets. It's not the slimmest or lightest but it charges my phone 4 times before I have to plug it in overnight. Has 2 ports so limits fighting between devices. Has come in handy often and do recommend.
